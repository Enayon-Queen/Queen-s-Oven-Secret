CAKES

Cakes are known to be sweet baked,commonly enjoyed for celebrations or as a treat. They come in various forms, from simple to elaborate, and are often decorated with frosting, icing, or other embellishments. Cakes are often made to celebrate special occasions like birthdays or weddings.These includes:red velvet cake,coffee cake,strawbery short cake and so on...

Classic Vanilla Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each. Stir in vanilla.
  5. Alternate adding flour mixture and milk to the batter, beginning and ending with flour.
  6. Divide batter into pans and bake for 25–30 minutes.
  7. Cool for 10 minutes in pans, then remove and cool completely.

Chocolate Fudge Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line two 9-inch pans.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. Whisk in eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Stir in hot coffee; batter will be thin.
  5. Pour into pans and bake 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before frosting.

Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp vinegar

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two cake pans.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, and cocoa.
  3. In another bowl, whisk oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  5. Divide into pans and bake 30 minutes.
  6. Cool and frost with cream cheese frosting.

Lemon Drizzle Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups self-rising flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup milk

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs one at a time.
  3. Stir in lemon zest and juice.
  4. Add flour and milk alternately, mixing until smooth.
  5. Bake 40–45 minutes or until golden.
  6. Drizzle with more lemon juice and sugar while warm.

Carrot Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease pans.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat sugar, oil, and eggs.
  4. Add dry ingredients, then carrots, pineapple, and nuts.
  5. Pour into pans and bake 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool and top with cream cheese frosting.

Banana Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
  • ½ cup buttermilk

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix in mashed bananas and buttermilk.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until combined.
  5. Pour into pan and bake 35–40 minutes.
  6. Cool before serving. Optional: top with cream cheese frosting.

Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 cups strawberries (sliced)
  • Whipped cream

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in butter until crumbly. Add milk to form dough.
  4. Roll out and cut into rounds. Bake 12–15 minutes.
  5. Split shortcakes, fill with strawberries and whipped cream.

Pineapple Upside-Down Cake

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • Pineapple slices
  • Maraschino cherries
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup milk

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Melt butter in a round pan and sprinkle with brown sugar.
  2. Arrange pineapple and cherries on top.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
  5. Add dry ingredients and milk alternately.
  6. Pour over fruit. Bake 35–40 minutes. Cool 10 mins then invert.

Coconut Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups cake flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp coconut extract
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup shredded coconut

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease pans.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and coconut extract.
  4. Alternate adding dry mix and coconut milk.
  5. Fold in shredded coconut. Pour into pans.
  6. Bake 30–35 minutes. Cool and frost with coconut frosting.

Coffee Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ cup brown sugar (topping)
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (topping)

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch pan.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients and sour cream alternately.
  5. Pour half into pan, sprinkle topping, then rest of batter.
  6. Bake 35–40 minutes. Cool before slicing.

Marble Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a pan.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt. Cream butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, mix well. Alternate flour and milk.
  4. Split batter in half. Stir cocoa into one half.
  5. Spoon both batters alternately in pan and swirl.
  6. Bake 40 minutes. Cool before serving.

Funfetti Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup rainbow sprinkles

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease pans.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar. Add egg whites and vanilla.
  4. Add flour mix and milk alternately.
  5. Fold in sprinkles. Pour into pans.
  6. Bake 25–30 minutes. Cool and frost as desired.
