COOKIES

Cookies are known to be a small,sweet baked goods,often crisp or chewy,made with flour,sugar,fat, and sometimes other ingredients like eggs or flavorings.they are various types of cookies and these include; Chocolate chip cookies, sugar cookies, oatmeal cookies

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden.

Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix dry ingredients, then combine with wet ingredients.
  4. Stir in oats and raisins.
  5. Drop onto baking sheet. Bake 10–12 minutes.

Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Roll into balls and flatten with fork.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes.

Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2.75 cups flour
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar + 2 tsp cinnamon (for rolling)

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs.
  3. Mix in dry ingredients.
  4. Shape dough into balls and roll in cinnamon sugar.
  5. Bake 8–10 minutes.

Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2.75 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Roll dough and cut with cookie cutters.
  6. Bake 8–10 minutes.

Double Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 0.75 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Preparation:

  1. Preheat to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix dry ingredients and combine.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips. Bake 10–12 mins.

Ginger Molasses Cookies

Ingredients

  • 0.75 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 cup molasses
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp ginger, 0.5 tsp cloves

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.

  3. Mix in dry ingredients. Roll in sugar.
  4. Bake 8–10 minutes.

Almond Biscotti Cookies

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup chopped almonds

Preparation:

  1. Preheat to 350°F.
  2. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and extract.
  3. Mix in flour and baking powder, then almonds.
  4. Shape into logs, bake 25 mins.
  5. Slice and bake again 10 mins each side.
