PANCAKES

A pancake, also known as a hotcake, is a flat type of batter bread like cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk, and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. we have various type of pancakes examples are

Classic Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredient

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 melted butter

Preparation:

  1. Whisk together dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a skillet, lightly grease, and pour 1/4 cup batter.
  5. Flip when bubbles form, cook until golden.

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Preparation:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, mashed banana, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently. Avoid overmixing.
  4. Heat a greased skillet and pour batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden on both sides.

Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Preparation:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, mashed banana, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently. Avoid overmixing.
  4. Heat a greased skillet and pour batter for each pancake.
  5. Gently fold in blueberries into the batter.
  6. Cook pancakes on a greased skillet until golden, flipping once bubbles appear.

Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips

Preparation:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, mashed banana, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently. Avoid overmixing.
  4. Heat a greased skillet and pour batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden
  6. Fold in chocolate chips carefully.
  7. Cook pancakes as usual on a greased skillet until golden.

Oatmeal Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter or oil

Preparation:

  1. Mix oats with flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter/oil.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir gently.
  4. Cook pancakes on a greased skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.

Lemon Ricotta Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp melted butter

Preparation

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk ricotta, milk, eggs, lemon zest, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently.
  4. Cook on a greased skillet until golden and cooked through.

Vegan Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup almond or soy milk
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Preparations:

  1. Mix milk and vinegar; let sit for 5 minutes to curdle (vegan "buttermilk").
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry, mix gently.
  4. Cook on a greased skillet until golden on both sides.

Savory Cheese Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Preparations:

  1. Mix flour,baking powder and salt together
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and fold in grated cheese.
  4. Cook on skillet until golden and cheese is melted inside.
